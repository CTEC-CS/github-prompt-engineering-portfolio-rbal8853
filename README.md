# Writing professional emails

>* This prompt helps people create emails

## Overview
This prompt produces a email for people who either don't have the time, or aren't great at writing emails

It solves the problem of people not being able to reach out because they cant communicate clearly
**Best For :**
- A student emailing a teacher about a grade
- A company manager emailing other workers about completing tasks
- Someone emailing a manager at a job to see if they can get a job there


**Output**
-
- A typical output looks like the email you will send out, with the persons name and your name
- ## Quick Start
### 1. Copy the prompt
I work at [ Where you work ] and am writing an email to [person you're emailing]
regarding [ what the email is about ] I would like it to be [ how many words in the email ].
Make the email [ friendly, professional, etc].
### 2. Fill in placeholders
- [Where you work] - what the email is for/ where you work
- [person you're emailing] - persons name and status
- [what the email is about] - what you are emailing the person for
- [how many words in the email] - how many words you would like the email to contain
- [friendly, professional, etc] - what you would like the mood of the email to be

## What Makes This Prompt Effective
**easy to use:**
The placeholders are clear and has good direction  

**Accurate:**
There is a lot of details so the AI knows almost exactly what you want  

**Reusable**
The prompt can be used for almost anything you need practice questions for


## Limitations & Considerations
**What This Prompt Does Well:**
- Clarity
- accuracy
- Usability
**What Requires Human Judgment:**
- Guidance
- deciphering the placeholders
**Always:**
- be clear in what you want
- accurately tell about your job/what you are emailing to
- give clear subjects

## Technical Details
**Framework:** RISE
**Optimal Model:** Google Gemini works well
**Average Response Length:** depends on how many you want, usually between 20-50 words
**Required Placeholders:** all of them if you want it to be accurate
